# Serious Leisure Scale (SLS) Questionnaire Structure

Source: Kim, M.-L. (2015). Reinventory of the Serious Leisure Scale (SLS II).

This file presents the structure of the questionnaire used in the study:
"Serious Leisure and Information Behavior in Creative Hobbies: The Case of Oil Painting Hobbyists"

The instrument consists of 24 items across six dimensions of serious leisure.

---

## Perseverance
| Item No. | Statement |
|---------|-----------|
| P1 | I do not give up on oil painting even when I encounter difficulties. |
| P2 | I try to continue oil painting despite physical or time-related challenges. |
| P3 | I find alternative ways to keep painting even when conditions are unfavorable. |
| P4 | I continue to make an effort even when I face obstacles. |

---

## Career
| Item No. | Statement |
|---------|-----------|
| C1 | I have specialized knowledge about oil painting techniques and materials. |
| C2 | I have the skills to create high-level oil paintings. |
| C3 | I have received special training or participated in unique opportunities related to oil painting. |
| C4 | People around me recognize my competence in oil painting. |

---

## Personal Effort
| Item No. | Statement |
|---------|-----------|
| E1 | I practice consistently to improve my oil painting skills. |
| E2 | I actively learn theory and background knowledge related to oil painting. |
| E3 | I set personal goals for my oil painting and strive to achieve them. |
| E4 | I make continuous efforts to improve my competence in oil painting. |

---

## Strong Identity
| Item No. | Statement |
|---------|-----------|
| I1 | Oil painting holds an important place in my life. |
| I2 | I feel a strong sense of identification with oil painting. |
| I3 | When introducing myself, I often mention oil painting as an important part of who I am. |
| I4 | Oil painting is an important part of my identity. |

---

## Unique Ethos
| Item No. | Statement |
|---------|-----------|
| U1 | I share a strong sense of connection with fellow oil painters. |
| U2 | I have strong bonds with other people who paint. |
| U3 | I recognize the unique culture shared by oil painting hobbyists. |
| U4 | I share similar values with others who participate in oil painting. |

---

## Durable Benefits and Rewards
| Item No. | Statement |
|---------|-----------|
| D1 | Oil painting brings vitality to my life. |
| D2 | I can express my thoughts and emotions through oil painting. |
| D3 | I feel a sense of accomplishment through oil painting. |
| D4 | Oil painting helps enhance my self-image and confidence. |

---

## Notes
- All items were measured using a 5-point Likert scale (1 = strongly disagree, 5 = strongly agree).
- Items are adapted from Kim (2015) Serious Leisure Scale (SLS II).
